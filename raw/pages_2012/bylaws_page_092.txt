                             SECTION 10
                    RADIO CONTRACT TERMS
            10.01. Mandatory Radio Contract Terms. All contracts
entered into by any Member of the Association for the broadcasting of
any of its games by radio shall contain provisions in the form required
by the Association:
            (a) Reserving to the Association the copyright in all
games broadcast; and
            (b) Rendering such contracts subject to (i) the
Constitution and By-Laws and all other rules, regulations, and
resolutions of the Association as they presently exist and as they may
from time to time be amended or modified; (ii) the terms of any
existing or future contracts entered into by the Association for the
broadcasting of basketball games; and (iii) the approval of the
Commissioner of the Association, with whom all contracts must be
filed within ten (10) days of their execution, and who may disapprove
such contracts, thereby rendering them null and void, only on the
ground that they fail to comply with the requirements of this Section
10.
