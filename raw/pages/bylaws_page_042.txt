                               ARTICLE 16
                             DISSOLUTION
            The Association may be dissolved at any time by a three
fourths (3/4) vote of all the members of the Board of Governors at a
meeting duly called for this purpose. Upon dissolution, the assets of
the Association shall be distributed in accordance with the laws of the
State of New York.
                               ARTICLE 17
                             AMENDMENTS
            (a) Except as otherwise provided for in this Constitution
and By-Laws, the Constitution and By-Laws of the Association may be
amended by the votes of three-fourths (3/4) of all the Governors at any
meeting of the Board of Governors, provided that the nature of the
proposed amendment is set forth in the Written Notice of the meeting.
            (b) The Constitution and By-Laws may also be amended
at any other meeting of the Board of Governors, without prior notice of
the amendment, provided there is complete attendance of all the
Governors and there is unanimous consent to the adoption of the
amendment at the meeting.
